Frequently Asked Questions

What are the requirements and procedures involved in acquiring a certified?

The office issues an application or requirement form in a format covering your Affidavits from parents attesting to the birth. This includes the child's Full name, Sex, Date of Birth, Place of Birth, Mother’s Maiden Name, Fathers Name, Father’s Religion, Father’s Occupation, Nationality of both parents, Applicant Phone Number, Name of one witness and GPS Address of Applicant.

How long does it take to acquire a certified true copy of birth certificates?

Depending on whether it is a new application or an old certificate. The duration for a new application is 4-6weeks. Converting an old certificate to a certified copy takes 10 working days

Where do I register for a death cert?

Relative of deceased must go to BDR Office in the District where the death occurred to register for a death certificate.

How much does it cost to obtain a certified true copy of birth certificates?

The legal fees on infant application attracts no cost, searches (old copies) cost Gh¢10.00 and late application attracts a penalty of GH¢20.00.

How do you retrieve a misplaced birth certificate?

This procedure involves a search on our system to check whether you had one and if it shows on our system as found, a copy is printed and submitted to the vault department to conduct their investigation and certifying that all the details on the copy has been rightfully entered in the register before we can retrieve a signed copy for you. This ensures the authenticity of the birth certificated issued out.

What is Particular Search in web development?

Where the applicant holds an old certificate and wants to replace it with a certified copy. The particular search only searches the record for Gh¢5. The print out of particular searches attracts extra Gh¢5. This makes the sum total for this service Gh¢10. Certified copies and particular searches are obtained at the head office.
